Privacy Policy

AlphaMagic — by iNiXR Technologies

Last updated: May 9, 2026

INIXR Technologies ("we", "us", or "our") operates the AlphaMagic mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding personal data when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App requests the CAMERA permission to display Augmented Reality (AR) experiences for kids. Camera frames are processed only on your device for real-time display. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any camera data, video, or images.

We do not collect:

  • ✓ Personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • ✓ Location data
  • ✓ Device identifiers (advertising ID, IMEI)
  • ✓ Usage analytics
  • ✓ Crash reports with personal data

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for children ages 3 to 8. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Designed for Families program. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from any user, including children under 13.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock all 26 letters and AR experiences. Payments are processed by Google Play Billing — we never receive your payment details. Refer to Google's Privacy Policy for how Google handles your purchase data.

Asset Downloads

After purchase, additional content is downloaded via Google Play Asset Delivery. This is handled entirely by Google's infrastructure. We do not see what you download or when.

Camera Permission Details

The CAMERA permission is required to render AR experiences in your real-world view. The camera feed is rendered live on screen but never recorded, saved, or transmitted off the device.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Billing — handles in-app purchases. Google's policy ↗
  • Google Play Asset Delivery — downloads optional content. Google's policy ↗
  • ARCore (by Google) — for AR functionality. Google's policy ↗

Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to lose. Any data the App stores (your premium-unlocked status, app preferences) is kept locally on your device using Android's built-in storage mechanisms.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date above.
